Welcome to Big Oak Cabin!

Big Oak Cabin is located at 2,200 feet, high on the ridgeline of Scenic Hwy 7, just a short walk from the most majestic overlooks in all the Ozark Mountain region. You can literally step out your door and see great distances to the west and all the way into Missouri to the northeast just a short walk down the road.

    Big Oak Cabin is a 2-level house, with 1800 square feet, and sleeps from 6 to 8 persons comfortably. A perfect getaway for you, a couple, even two couples or even a large family. Two bedrooms / two baths.
  • The downstairs master bedroom has a king bed.
  • The upstairs bedroom has a queen bed and a double bed.
  • The upstairs living area has a "day bed", with a roll-away bed underneath.
