Rates and Reservations

Pricing

  • $100 / night for 2 persons (2 night minimum)
  • $10 extra each additional person

Payment

We accept all major credit cards: VISA, MasterCard, Discover, American Express

  • Big Oak Cabin is completely furnished, including cooking utensils, table service and flatware. There are extra linens, towels, bathroom tissue and soap in each bath and a washer and dryer are provided, for your laundry. Maid service cleaning is done after your departure and is not provided daily for extended stays. But if you need anything just let us know. 870-446-2810

Cancellation Policy

Deposits are refundable only if 14 days advance notice is given before your arrival.

Once you have confirmed a reservation, a deposit is refunded only if 14 days advance Notice is given.

If you do not call to cancel and do not show up for your reservation, you are responsible for the Full amount of your stay.
